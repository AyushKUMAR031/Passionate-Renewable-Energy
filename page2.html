Solar Energy.

Solar energy is the energy produced by the sun, which is converted into electricity using solar panels. It is a renewable and sustainable source of energy that does not produce emissions or waste. Solar energy can be used to power homes, businesses, and vehicles, and is a key component of a sustainable energy future. It helps to reduce dependence on fossil fuels and mitigate the impacts of climate change.

  • Solar energy is produced by the sun and converted into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) cells.
  • It is a renewable and sustainable source of energy that does not produce emissions or waste.
  • Solar energy costs have decreased over the past decade, making them more affordable.
  • Solar energy systems can be installed on rooftops, land, or solar farms.
  • Solar energy systems can be grid-tied, off-grid, or hybrid.
  • The solar energy industry is growing rapidly, with research focused on improving efficiency and expanding its use in different applications.
